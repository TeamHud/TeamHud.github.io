RWDC Summery

The RWDC challenge scenario state's that their will be 2 billion more people on earth and we will need 70% more food. The challenge wants us to come up with done to accurately disperse pesticides. They want the fastests way and biggest load of pesticides but saving cost. The challenge focuses on unmanned drone types. You need two unmanned systems or be able to modify the first system to complete this set of tasks. Each team needs to have the ability to analyze tasks, strategize, design, cost management, and incorporate alternative uses. Each member of the team needs to consider and fully understand the various interactions, dependencies, and limitations of an unmanned system. This is help further the capabilities of drones and how to upgrade them. The task is to find the best way to deploy pesticides while eliminating cost of lots of them. The group that builds the best drones for the job will win. Also the best tech document of the team's activities will also get an award. So it is important to keep track of whatever any member of a person’s respective team is. The area to spread the pesticides is a 2 acre by 2 acre corn field. The uav needs to be able to carry a large amount of pesticide as well as have enough battery to fly across the entire field. The drone design needs to be focused on efficiency and minimizing costs.
